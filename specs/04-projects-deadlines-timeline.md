# Projects, deadlines & timeline

Project:
- client_name, project_name, total_qty, start_date(optional), final_deadline(optional)

Per ProjectStage:
- planned_due_date (required in MVP)
- actual_ready_at
- actual_started_at
- actual_done_at
- qty_required (default = project total_qty, editable)
- qty_done (starts at 0)

Timeline:
- Show per stage: planned_due_date vs actual timestamps, duration, delays.
